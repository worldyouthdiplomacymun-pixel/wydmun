About Us

Building the Next Generation
of Global Leaders

World Youth Diplomacy Model United Nations is a student-run MUN conference we aim to bring together passionate young leaders from across the globe to address the defining issues of our time.

Founded by students, for students, our aim is to create an environment where students are challenged to think critically and collborate across cultures, directly simulating real world diplomacy.

By addressing global issues around the world, it allows the free-spirited opinions of the youth to be heard and aids in the development of creative resolutions. Join us for our first annual conference in 2026... online!
